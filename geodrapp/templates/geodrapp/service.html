Training, Research and Consultancy

Geospatial Development and Research Hub offers comprehensive services and operations in three main segments, namely training, research and consultancy centred on geospatial technology as a core business tool. In training, research and consulting, the company implements and builds up its services in GIS, Remote Sensing, Web mapping, Geocoding, Maching Learning, Surveying, Data science, Web development.

  • Training: GIS, Remote sensing, Web mapping, Geocoding, Maching Learning, Land Surveying, Data Science, web Development

    • Training will be based on the development of programs and applications on Esri products (ArcGIS Desktop, ArcGIS online, ArcIMS, ArcView, Collector, Survey 123, ArcGIS pro), programming languages (Python, Java script, R, and C++), open source software (QGIS, JOSM, and others), surveying, web design, database management, data science, digital and web mapping, geocoding, use of GPS.

  • Research: GIS, Remote sensing, Web mapping, Geocoding, Maching Learning, Land Surveying, Data Science, web Development

    • Research in GeoDR Hub is conducted on behalf of the company itself and or on behalf of clients, partners or the community where geospatial technology is applied to meet the desired outcome. Like training, research activities in GeoDR Hub cover the disciplines of GIS, Remote Sensing, Web mapping, Geocoding, Maching Learning, Surveying, Data science, Web development.

  • Consultancy: GIS, Remote sensing, Web mapping, Geocoding, Maching Learning, Land Surveying, Data Science, web Development

    • The consultancy service will cover GIS and Remote Sensing, master plan development, research, land use mapping, agricultural, cadastral maps, topographic surveys, environmental and social impact assessment (ESIA), resettlement action plan, spatial data collection (GPS data collection, drone, Lidar survey, etc.), data collection (survey), climate change mitigation and adaptation.

  • Research grant application GIS, Remote sensing, Web mapping, Geocoding, Maching Learning, Land Surveying, Data Science, web Development

    • Doing research is all about profit, money or other forms of satisfaction. Being able to perform well and conduct effective research, we apply for different opportunities to get sponsorship so as to carry out the research. Research will not be limited to any one field, every possible opportunity can be exploited.
