We offer: Training, Research and Consultancy in geoinformatics and adjacent fields applying the latest technologies

Geospatial Development and Research Hub - GeoDR, is a geospatial based company working across all the fields while applying geospatial technology to meet the needs of people. It serves customers on site, as well as online across Rwanda and the rest of the world. It's an established geospatial, coding and research hub focusing on core principles to emerge as one of the most enlightened companies in offering extensive services. GeoDR Hub, provides a complete spectrum of Geographic Information System (GIS) and technology driven environmental solutions to both clients from the public and private sectors.
