Overview

Geospatial Development and Research Hub (GeoDR Hub), founded in May 2021, is a limited liability company, legally registered in the Republic of Rwanda according to Article 16 of Law No.07/2009 of 27/04/2009 regulating companies in Rwanda. It is a leading geospatial, coding and research hub that focuses on the fundamentals to emerge as one of the most enlightened companies in offering extensive consultancy services, client-based research services as well as training and capacity building on GIS and remote sensing, cartographic programming languages, land surveying, web design, database management, data science, digital and web mapping and geo-coding. The company provides a full spectrum of geographic information systems (GIS) and technology-driven environmental solutions to both public and private sector clients.

Being a client-focused company, the commitment is to provide cost-effective solutions to the clients, continuously looking for the best and latest technological solutions for the improvement of our environment, and to support them from initiation to completion in their endeavours. GeoDR team is a joint effort of highly focused and dedicated professionals with the experience to lead high-tech teams to perform all services. It shares extensive experience in performing significant tasks in the area of GIS, land surveying, environmental studies, watershed planning, cadastral studies, and so forth. The company has 9 co-founding members and about 20 temporary staff including land surveyors, GIS and remote sensing experts, urban planners, environmentalists, enumerators and surveyors for occasional work.

Historical background and origin

Geospatial Development and Research Hub (GeoDR Hub) has officially started operation on 00/00/2021 with 9 co-founding members. The idea to build the Hub was born 2 years after graduating in Geography and Urban Planning. With a strong ambition to bring into practice what was learned in the college, we found very limited opportunities to apply this knowledge to earn money. Both of us wanted to develop our knowledge specifically in the field of geographic information systems and use it to combat the problems that society faces. Indeed, many problems need the application of the latest knowledge to address causes and consequences and provide solutions. Geospatial information technology should be used for decision-making, as it is exactly what society needs today to achieve sustainable development.

Since it was realised that we have this knowledge but limited opportunities to get a supporting entity to apply it on ground, we thought we would join forces and implement what we have in mind. We held many discussions and meetings and decided to build a strong team to provide geospatial technology services. These ideas came to fruition in 2020, but due to the Covid 19 pandemic, the start of the project was temporarily halted and did not resume until 2021. However, after a period of ups and downs, various discussions were undertaken and it was decided to establish a geospatial services company. The dream came true when we completed the agreement and the company was officially initiated on 00/00/2021 with 9 co founders as mentioned at the beginning of this section. This happened after we came to realise that the benefits of having our own business in this field outweigh those of finding a job and will give us satisfaction and success in life for a long time.

In summary, we strongly believe that setting up our own business in the field of geospatial computing will help us advance in our careers and give us lifelong satisfaction rather than choosing to work for others.

Name and Motto

Name: GeoDR Hub | Geospatial Development and Research Hub

Motto: "Putting real life on a map" This motto was put in place for the reason that the company is taking real life problems, and bringing them into the situation then using geospatial technology, as well as cartographic analysis to address them, tackle their causes, and provide solutions towards achieving sustainable development. What makes us so special is that we are mapping the life around the world to bring the solution to the problem to support the development and prosperity of human being.

The logo

The logo was designed to represent the complete icon of the company - GeoDR Hub. It combines all the symbolic elements of the company and their meanings correspond to the mission and vision. The complete logo means applying geospatial technology in research to achieve sustainable development, as well as to meet the needs of the community. The entire concept of the logo is intended to lead to a broader unity within the company and outside.
