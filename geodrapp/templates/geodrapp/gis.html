GIS (Geographic Information System)
  • It is a system designed to capture, store, manipulate, analyze, manage and present spatial or geographic data.
  • GIS can relate the unrelated information using the location as the key index variable. It is a powerful and useful tool for monitoring, management and prediction of spatial complex phenomena. It is also a support tool to decision making which is more used now. In brief, GIS is much more than a map.
